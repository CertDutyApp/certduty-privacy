Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

CertDuty ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using CertDuty, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use CertDuty, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile Information: Legal name, city, county
  • Authentication Data: Sign in with Apple or Google account credentials
  • License Information: License numbers, types, issue dates, expiration dates, issuing authorities
  • Training Records: Training completion dates, certificate information, instructor details
  • Documents: Uploaded certificates, training records, and related documents (PRO subscribers only)
  • Firearms Information: For CCW licenses - make, model, serial number, caliber (stored securely)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features accessed, time spent in app, interactions with features
  • Error and Diagnostic Data: Crash reports, performance data, error logs

1.3 Location Information

We collect location information (city and county) that you provide during onboarding. We do not track your precise real-time location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Services: Track license compliance, calculate renewal dates, manage training records
  • Send Notifications: Alert you about upcoming license expirations, training deadlines, and requalification requirements (PRO subscribers only)
  • Process Subscriptions: Manage your PRO subscription, process payments, and provide premium features
  • Store Documents: Securely store and retrieve your uploaded certificates and training documents (PRO subscribers only)
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Meet regulatory requirements and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate CertDuty:

3.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Authentication, database storage, file storage, crash analytics
  • Data Shared: Account information, license data, uploaded documents
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy

3.2 RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Subscription management and entitlement tracking
  • Data Shared: User ID, subscription status, purchase information
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy

3.3 Apple Services

  • Sign in with Apple: Authentication (you control what information is shared)
  • In-App Purchases: Subscription billing through App Store
  • Push Notifications: Expiration and deadline alerts

3.4 Google Sign-In

  • Purpose: Alternative authentication method
  • Data Shared: Name, email address

4. Subscription Information

Auto-Renewable Subscriptions:
  • Pricing: $4.99 per month or $49.99 per year
  • Features: Unlimited licenses, document storage, push notifications, priority support
  • Billing: Charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase
  • Auto-Renewal: Subscription automatically renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • Renewal Charge: Account will be charged within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
  • Management: Manage subscriptions and turn off auto-renewal in Account Settings after purchase
  • Cancellation: Cancel anytime through your App Store account settings
  • No Refunds: No refunds for partial subscription periods

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Firebase Security Rules: Access controls ensure users can only access their own data
  • Secure Authentication: Industry-standard authentication protocols
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to user data
  • Regular Updates: We keep our security measures up to date

Important: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide you services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we must retain it for legal compliance.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your personal information in the app's Profile settings.

7.2 Delete Account

You can request account deletion by contacting us at support@certduty.com. This will permanently delete your data.

7.3 Notification Preferences

PRO subscribers can manage notification settings per license within the app.

7.4 Marketing Communications

We currently do not send marketing emails. If we do in the future, you can opt out anytime.

7.5 Do Not Track

We do not track users across third-party websites and do not respond to Do Not Track signals.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@certduty.com.

9. Children's Privacy

CertDuty is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a child, contact us immediately.

10. International Users

CertDuty is designed for users in the United States, specifically California. If you access the app from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.
